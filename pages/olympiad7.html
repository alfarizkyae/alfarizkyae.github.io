Matriks Mathematics Olympiad (MMO) — 2nd Winner

Matriks Math Olympiad (MMO) is a national mathematics competition organized by the Mathematics Education Department of Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang (UMM). The competition brings together talented students from across Indonesia to solve challenging olympiad-style problems that emphasize logical reasoning, mathematical creativity, and advanced problem-solving skills in algebra, geometry, combinatorics, and number theory. MMO aims to foster academic excellence and promote interest in mathematics through a competitive and intellectually stimulating environment.
